The Earth Illusion

A natural parallax illusion: as you move your head, scroll, or shift your gaze, the Earth and its clouds appear to drift against a star field. This effect emerges from depth cues and relative motion—your brain interprets layered displacement as real spatial depth, even on a flat screen.
