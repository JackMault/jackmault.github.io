Perceptual Stewardship and Identity Refinement: The MIIR Instrument

Jack Mault •

This extended abstract introduces MIIR, the lab’s first instrument for articulating identity as a measurable, refinable model. Drawing on self-concept clarity, narrative identity, and participatory co-modeling, MIIR helps participants externalize claims, strengthen alignment, and carry the shift forward through symbolic anchors like portraiture.
