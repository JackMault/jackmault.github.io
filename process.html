The MIIR Process

Seven moves that turn an internal conversation into a visible change.

1) Prime
“What are you trying to grow — in yourself or in the world — and how will you know when you see it?”
2) Serialize (Round 1)
Practitioner translates dialogue into concise identity statements.
3) Plot (RTSS 1)
Participant plots confidence/time positioning.
4) Disrupt
Evaluate instability and noise; challenge misfit identities.
5) Serialize (Round 2)
Sparser, value‑aligned set emerges.
6) Plot (RTSS 2)
Visible consolidation and confidence shift.
7) Embody
Paired portraits capture the new posture.
